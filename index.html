Privacy Policy

Your data, your control.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is needed to run the app:

  • Account info: Email address, display name, username, and optional profile photo
  • Content: Challenge videos, proof videos, and thumbnails you upload
  • Activity: Likes, comments, follows, challenge participation, and voting data
  • Scores: Trust score, speed scores, streak days, and completion count

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account and send OTP verification emails
  • Display your profile, challenges, and proofs to other users
  • Calculate scores, streaks, and leaderboard rankings
  • Send in-app notifications for challenge activity
  • Moderate content through community voting and report review

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security enabled on every table
  • Videos and images stored in Supabase Storage, served via Cloudflare CDN
  • Server region: ap-southeast-1 (Singapore)
  • Authentication via Supabase Auth with OTP email verification
  • Passwords are hashed - we never store or see plain-text passwords

4. Content Moderation

  • Automated keyword filtering blocks violent, hateful, sexual, and dangerous content
  • Pattern filtering blocks phone numbers, email addresses, and URLs in challenge content
  • Community voting: proof videos are upvoted/downvoted by users. Content with 70%+ downvotes (minimum 5 votes) may be flagged for review
  • Users can report content for 8 reasons including copyright infringement, violence, and harassment
  • Violations may result in content removal or account suspension

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and file storage
  • Cloudflare: CDN for video delivery
  • Firebase: Push notifications (planned for future release)
  • Google Fonts: Typography (no personal data collected)

6. Your Rights

  • Deactivate: Temporarily disable your account from Settings. Reactivate anytime by logging in.
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all data from Settings. This action is irreversible.
  • Block: Block other users to prevent interaction.
  • Report: Report content that violates our community guidelines.

7. DMCA & Copyright

Users must own all content they upload. For DMCA takedown requests, email doitapp.twilighthawk@gmail.com. We respond within 48 hours. Repeat offenders face account termination.

8. Children's Privacy

DoIt! is rated for Teen (13+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not display ads
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics beyond basic app functionality
  • We do not share your email with marketers
